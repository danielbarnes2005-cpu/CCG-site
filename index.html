About Us

Open to All.
Built for the Bold.

We're not a business school club — we're a university-wide community. Whether you're a STEM major, a humanities student, or anywhere in between, if you think critically and want to solve hard problems, you belong here.

Our mission is to help GSU students discover the many fields of consulting, build real skills through hands-on practice, and forge the relationships needed to launch a career at the world's top firms.

  • We teach you how to think like a consultant, not just what consulting is.
  • Live case workshops, real client-style projects, and honest recruiting prep.
  • A growing alumni network placing students at MBB and top-tier firms.

What We Do

Programs &
Offerings

Every program is designed to close the gap between classroom theory and real consulting practice.

Case Workshops

Hands-on sessions where members work through real business cases with structured frameworks, group problem-solving, and expert feedback — the closest thing to a real interview.

Industry Panels

Conversations with professionals across strategy consulting, corporate strategy, and adjacent fields — candid, practical, and focused on what actually matters for your career.

Recruiting Events

Firm-specific info sessions, coffee chats, and networking events to help you build relationships that matter before application season opens.

Professional Development

Resume reviews, behavioral prep, and skill-building sessions designed to make you competitive — whether you're targeting MBB, Big 4, or boutique strategy firms.
